Welcome to the generator!

×

The function is easy:

  • Press the space bar to generate some random type combinations
  • On the left menu you can change the generator parameters, so when you press space will only appear the combinations you want
  • On the right menu you can change the font family for each text
  • On the top you can change the view
  • To edit the text you can click directly on it. The space bar will not generate will focused on the text.

This is not a finished product, so most of the options are disabled.
